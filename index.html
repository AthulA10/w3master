Who are We?

Web3Kerala is a social community that connects everyone on Web3 in Kerala in a contextually meaningful way. The community provides opportunity for individuals in the Web3 space in Kerala to connect and uplift each and every member in the community.With an objective to setup a Web3 ecosystem in Kerala to provide education and networking opportunities to Web3Kerala members to build,grow, and scale Web3.

Mission

Democratize web3 knowledge ,resources & accessibility and build kerala’s largest Web3 community.

Vision

To provide education, job & networking opportunities to Kerala web3 enthusiast to build , grow and scale in web3.

Goals

To make Kerala a Web3 hub.

Purpose of WEB3KERALA
